How to Use This Map

Selecting a Year

Tap the year at the top to view results from different elections (1994–2025).

Exploring Districts

Tap any district to see detailed results. The map zooms in automatically.

Pinch to Zoom

Use two fingers to pinch-zoom the map. Drag with one finger to pan.

Viewing Statistics

Use the buttons on the right for national totals or districts won per party.

Searching Districts

Use the search button to find specific districts quickly.

Navigating Years

Use Prev/Next Year buttons or tap the header to pick a year.

District Labels

Toggle labels on/off via "Show Labels" in the year dropdown.
